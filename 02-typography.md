# Typography

## Families

- **Headings:** Roboto Condensed, Bold weight.
- **Body:** Kumbh Sans, Regular weight.

Both are self-hosted on the Shopify Horizon theme via WOFF2 files in the Assets folder, with CSS variable overrides. Keep to these two families. Icons pair with Kumbh Sans; never introduce a third typeface to sit beside them.

## Scale

Web sizes shown as em / pt / px. Additional sizes are permitted where layout needs them, such as product labelling and advertising.

| Role | Family | em | pt | px |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Title / H1 | Roboto Condensed Bold | 3.05 | 36.6 | 55 |
| Subtitle / H2 | Roboto Condensed Bold | 2.44 | 29 | 44 |
| Heading / H3 | Roboto Condensed Bold | 1.95 | 23.4 | 35 |
| Subheading / H4 | Roboto Condensed Bold | 1.56 | 18.7 | 28 |
| Section Header / H5 | Roboto Condensed Bold | 1.25 | 15 | 23 |
| Body | Kumbh Sans Regular | 1.0 | 12 | 18 |
| Quote | Kumbh Sans Regular | 1.25 | 15 | 20 |
| Caption | Kumbh Sans Regular | 0.8 | 9.6 | 14 |
| Small print | Kumbh Sans Regular | 0.64 | 7.7 | 12 |

## Heading colour ramp

Headings step down through the ink ramp rather than all sitting on the same black. Body sits on Ink Body.

- H1 Ink Black `#161617`
- H2 Ink Deep `#3F4042`
- H3 Ink Strong `#414245`
- H4 Ink Mid `#4B4C4F`
- Body Ink Body `#494A4D`

## Usage

- Roboto Condensed Bold is the brand's voice at volume: headings, the product-style wordmarks (FLOW / CORE / DEEP), sizes and short labels. Its condensed, confident cut carries the sober register well.
- Kumbh Sans does the reading: body, ingredient descriptions, captions, small print. On labels it runs as small as 4.5pt for legal text, so keep line length and spacing generous wherever it is small.
- The tagline SkinBiomeNourishment is set in Kumbh Sans with *Biome* in bold and the rest regular.
