# Open questions

Things this system has surfaced that are not yet settled. Recorded here rather than quietly decided, so the loose threads stay visible.

## Colour

- **Natural / Naturally Derived icon token.** The guidelines artwork draws this icon in green, but no named colour token was ever assigned. Recommendation: Meadow `#7BB65C`, the one green not otherwise spoken for. Watch the proximity to Gorse `#68903C` (Climate Clear); keep the two green icons from touching without separation.
- **Style ramp confirmation.** Tide is chosen. Driftwood and Stone are kept as saved alternatives in `01-colour.md`.

## Icons

- **The ethos set: five or eight.** The Graphics page of the guidelines states the ethos set is "exactly and only" the five originals and is fixed. The Icons page shows eight. The five-only statement is out of date; this system treats the set as eight, with the three additions marked newer. The guidelines wording needs reconciling.
- **Additions on the label or not.** Whether the three additional icons (Natural, Science Led, Ethical Standards) appear on the physical label or stay web and marketing only is undecided. The Product Packaging and Label Design spec needs updating to match whatever is chosen.
- **Icon style versus ethos artwork.** The stated icon style is single-weight outline, no fill and no shading. The ethos icons carry selective solid fills and colour. This system treats the ethos set as a deliberate exception and holds other icons to the outline rule, but the guidelines could say so explicitly.

## Wording and source drift

- **"Near Welcombe" in older docs.** Some source documents still read "near Welcombe" and need correcting to "in Welcombe".
- **Brand Voice canonical version.** The Canva and Google Doc versions of the Brand Voice document have drifted. One needs to be named canonical.
- **Ethos icon captions and fragrance.** Confirm no ethos caption implies fragrance-free or essential-oil-free. The correct claim is "no synthetic fragrance".
